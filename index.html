Dao De Jing

The Dao De Jing, also known as the Tao Te Ching, is a classical Chinese text attributed to Laozi. Written over 2,500 years ago, it explores the philosophy of Dao (the Way) and De (virtue), guiding individuals in harmony, balance, and self-cultivation. Each of its 81 chapters offers wisdom for modern life, mindfulness, and inner clarity.
